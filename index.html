About us

Welcome to Summit Haven, the ultimate destination for hiking enthusiasts. Nestled in the heart of majestic mountains, our town offers breathtaking trails for all skill levels. Immerse yourself in serene wilderness, join our vibrant hiking community, and discover a haven for outdoor adventure. Beyond hiking, enjoy mountain biking, rock climbing, and more. Unwind in our charming downtown, where local shops and eateries await. Summit Haven: Where Every Step is a Journey, and Every Journey is an Adventure. Embark on a journey of discovery as you traverse our extensive network of hiking trails. From leisurely strolls for beginners to challenging ascents for seasoned hikers, Summit Haven offers a diverse range of trails suitable for all skill levels. As you meander through lush forests, cross babbling streams, and ascend to breathtaking vistas, you'll experience the awe-inspiring beauty of nature at every turn. After a day of exploration, unwind in our charming downtown area. Discover quaint shops, local eateries, and vibrant cultural events that showcase the town's unique charm. Summit Haven is not just a place to hike; it's a destination where you can experience the perfect blend of adventure and relaxation. Come to Summit Haven and let the trails guide you to new heights. Whether you're a seasoned hiker or just beginning your journey, our town welcomes you with open arms. Immerse yourself in the beauty of nature, connect with a vibrant community, and make memories that will last a lifetime. Summit Haven: Where Every Step is a Journey, and Every Journey is an Adventure.
